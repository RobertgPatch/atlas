# Spec 003 - Review and Finalization

## Goal
Provide review-first validation for K-1 corrections, mapping, approval, and finalization.

## Scope
- K-1 review workspace
- issue queue
- corrections
- approval
- finalization

## Functional Requirements
- review parsed fields side-by-side with PDF
- map entity and partnership
- confirm reported distribution from Box 19A
- block finalization until requirements are met
- create/update annual activity row on finalization

## Acceptance Criteria
- finalize blocked until complete + approved
- review actions persist
- finalization is audited
